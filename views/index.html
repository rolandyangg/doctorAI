Our Mission


Millions of people across the world lack basic health care and/or can't even afford it. Furthermore, with the COVID-19 Pandemic, medical workers have been in extremely high demand, with there being not enough to go around for everyone. We propose an AI solution that could be a helper to and potential substitue for medical workers, so everyone can receive the treatment they deserve while workers can focus more on saving lives.

Acessibility


Most people don't have a MD in their family, but what most people do have is access to the internet. With just a camera and a mic, people can receive a wellness visit.
